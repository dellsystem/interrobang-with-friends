how it works

Connect with your fridge
  1. Make friends with penguins Mandatory step.
  2. Select friends to punctuate You can anonymously send each friend a "!" or a "?".
  3. Let the interrobanging happen If your friend responds with the opposite character, the "!" and "?" will magically combine, resulting in a ‽.

privacy

DOWN‽ was created to avoid awkward punctuation, so rest assured we don't really care about privacy.

Secure

Your "!"s and "?"s are encrypted using state-of-the-art double ROT13 encryption technology.

Anonymous

By default, we make sure you don't show up as an interrobanger in any searches on FridgeBook.
